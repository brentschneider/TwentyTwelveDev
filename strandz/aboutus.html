About Strandz

Our talented and professional stylists can bring your beauty ideal to life. We can take your curly hair to straight, your straight hair to curly, or anywhere in between!


Quincy McDonald

Owner / Stylist

Training: Mr. Leon's School of Hair Design 1991 / Redken Specialist Program 1996 / Advanced Extension Certification 2002.

 

I opened Strandz in 2000. After working as a stylist for larger corporations, I felt the need to open a small intimate full service salon. I wanted to offer ethnic hair care services, as it was very difficult to find other salons in Spokane that did. Either the stylists were scared because of inexperience or the training simply wasn’t offered.

 

"Hair can be like a puzzle or riddle. I love to solve, put together, and unlock beauty of women who are looking for something new, and may not know what it is!" - Quincy

 

Kristen Sims:

Stylist / Eyelash Technician

Training: Training: Toni & Guy Hairdressing 2010, Eyelash extension Training 2010, Hair Extension Training 2010, Keratin Complex by Coppola 2010, Affinage Color Class 2010 & 2011

 

Kristen enjoys working with each client and helping them understand their hair and how they can best work with it.

 

Quincy has always been my biggest supporter and mentor. Working with Ethnic hair has always been a dream of mine and at Strandz I have been able to fulfill my dream and am getting better and better with every client. I started off my career in Coeur D’ Alene at Bling Salon but I soon found the fast paced salon was not my style. I enjoy sitting down with my clients and completing a consultation before enhancing my clients true beauty.

 

" Draw a crazy picture, write a nutty poem, sing a mumble-gumble song, whistle through your comb. Do a bone dance across the salon floor, put something silly in the world that aint been there before." -Kristen
